Practical Info

How to reach the festival, what to expect, and who to contact.

How to Get Here

Public transport: Take a train to Innsbruck and continue by regional bus or festival shuttle.

Bike: Bike parking is available close to the entrance, and the area has scenic cycling routes.

Car: Follow the festival signs in Tirol. Parking is limited, so carpooling is recommended.

Plane: The closest airports are Innsbruck, Munich, and Salzburg. From there, continue by train or bus.

Location Map

The festival takes place in Tirol, Austria.

Rules and Notes

  • No outside alcohol.
  • Reusable cups are used on site.
  • Children must stay with an adult.
  • Respect the quiet zones and walking paths.
  • Drones, fireworks, and open flames are not allowed.

Contact

Email: hello@melodiesofpeace.org

Social: @melodiesofpeace

Help desk: Open from 09:00 until 20:00 on festival days.

Emergency note: Follow staff directions and the on-site signage if weather changes quickly in the mountains.
